## 4. Posting Schedule

### Recommended Frequency
- **Weeks 1–4 (Launch):** 5–8 pins per day
- **Weeks 5–12 (Growth):** 3–5 pins per day
- **Ongoing (Maintenance):** 2–4 pins per day

Pinterest rewards consistency over volume. Spreading pins throughout the day is better than posting them all at once.

### Best Times to Post (UK Audience)
| Priority | Time (GMT/BST) | Reason |
|----------|---------------|--------|
| **Best** | 20:00–22:00 | UK evening browsing peak — people research purchases after dinner |
| **Strong** | 12:00–14:00 | Lunch break browsing |
| **Good** | 07:00–09:00 | Morning commute/browsing |
| **Avoid** | 00:00–06:00 | Minimal UK engagement |

Spread pins across 2–3 time slots per day for best results.

### First 30 Days Strategy

**Week 1: Foundation (Days 1–7)**
- Set up profile, claim website, enable Rich Pins
- Create all 8 boards with descriptions
- Pin 5–6 pins per day — start with the strongest content:
  - Best Home EV Chargers UK 2026 (all 3 variations)
  - Top reviews: Ohme, Pod Point, Zappi, Andersen
  - Key guides: Installation Cost, Charging Cost, OZEV Grant
- Total: ~35–40 pins published

**Week 2: Expand (Days 8–14)**
- Pin 6–8 per day — cover remaining reviews and comparisons
- Mix content types: 2 reviews + 2 guides + 2 buyer guides per day
- Total: ~42–56 pins published (cumulative: ~80–96)

**Week 3: Fill Out (Days 15–21)**
- Pin 5–6 per day — remaining guides and comparison pins
- All 123 pins should be live by end of week 3
- Begin re-pinning highest-performing pins from week 1

**Week 4: Optimise (Days 22–30)**
- Review Pinterest Analytics — which pins get clicks?
- Re-pin top performers to different relevant boards
- Start planning "fresh" pins — new variations of best-performing content
- Drop to 3–4 pins per day

### Re-Pinning Strategy
- **Wait 3+ months** before re-pinning the same image to the same board
- **Cross-board pinning:** Pin each piece of content to 2–3 relevant boards (not all at once — space by 1–2 days)
- **Seasonal refresh:** Create new pin images for top content every quarter
- **Fresh content rule:** Pinterest prioritises new images. When re-promoting old articles, create a new pin image with a different text overlay — don't just re-pin the same image

### Content Calendar Rotation
To keep the feed varied, follow this daily mix:
- 1–2 Review pins
- 1 Comparison pin
- 1 Guide/educational pin
- 1 Buyer guide pin

## 5. Pin Image Guidelines

### Dimensions
- **Optimal size:** 1000 × 1500 px (2:3 ratio)
- **Minimum width:** 600 px
- **Maximum aspect ratio:** 1:2.5 (avoid longer — Pinterest crops in feeds)
- **File format:** PNG or high-quality JPG
- **Max file size:** 20 MB

### Brand Colours (Suggested)
| Element | Colour | Hex |
|---------|--------|-----|
| Primary background | Dark charcoal/navy | #1A1A2E or #16213E |
| Accent / highlight | Electric green or teal | #0F9D58 or #00BFA5 |
| Text (on dark) | White | #FFFFFF |
| Text (on light) | Dark grey | #2D2D2D |
| Secondary accent | Amber/orange (for ratings) | #F59E0B |

### Visual Template Styles

**Template A: Review Pin**
- Dark background with product image
- Large bold text overlay (charger name + "UK Review")
- Star rating graphic (e.g. ★★★★★ 4.9/5)
- Logistryx logo/URL at bottom
- Badge for awards: "Best Overall", "Best Value", "Best for Solar"

**Template B: Comparison Pin**
- Split design — two chargers side by side
- "VS" graphic in the centre
- Bold question: "Which one wins?"
- Key stats for each (price, rating)
- Logistryx branding at bottom

**Template C: Guide/Educational Pin**
- Clean, minimal background (white or light grey)
- Bold headline text at top
- 3–4 bullet points or numbered list as teaser
- Icon or illustration (⚡🔌☀️💷)
- "Read the full guide" call-to-action

**Template D: Money-Saving Pin**
- Green/teal background
- Large number or stat ("Save £400/year", "7p/kWh")
- Brief explanatory text
- Arrow or CTA pointing to "Read more"

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Bold sans-serif (e.g. Montserrat, Poppins, or Inter — bold/extra-bold)
- **Body text:** Same family, regular weight
- **Size:** Headline should be readable at mobile thumbnail size (minimum 40pt equivalent)
- **Maximum 3 text elements per pin** — don't overcrowd

### General Design Rules
1. **Keep text to 20–30% of the image** — Pinterest penalises text-heavy pins
2. **Use high-contrast colours** — readable at thumbnail size
3. **Include the Logistryx logo or URL** on every pin (subtle, bottom corner)
4. **Use real product images** where possible — stock photos feel generic
5. **Consistent branding** — every pin should be recognisably "Logistryx" at a glance
6. **No clip art** — clean, professional aesthetic
7. **Test readability** — view your pin at mobile size before publishing

### Tools for Creating Pins
- **Canva Pro** (recommended) — create brand templates, schedule directly to Pinterest
- **Adobe Express** — alternative with good Pinterest templates
- **Tailwind** — scheduling tool with smart scheduling and pin design features
